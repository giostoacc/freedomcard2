Questions, answered

Is Freedom Card a bank?

No. Freedom Card partners with regulated EU banking providers so you can keep your existing account. We focus on proactive goal enforcement layered on top of your current setup, while your deposits remain safeguarded by the statutory guarantees of those EU banks.

How do you protect my data?

We are GDPR-first: minimal data collection, encryption at rest, granular consent controls, and an always-on data deletion request inside the app.

When will the beta launch?

We are onboarding limited cohorts throughout 2025 across Ireland, Portugal, Germany, Spain, and more—Freedom Card will be available across the EU. Joining the waitlist reserves your priority spot.

Do I have to move my salary?

No. You simply fund the card from your current account. We automate transfers according to your savings rules and can pause anytime.
